About Me

Hi! I'm Stephanie

"A jack of all trades is a master of none, but oftentimes better than a master of one."

Originally from New York , raised in Georgia (Go Dawgs) , based in Washington D.C.

My interests in web development started like every other millenial, through MySpace. I realized how much I enjoyed problem solving and developed my own creative style.

All of that took a backseat after I became a Delta Flight Attendant for a few years and travelled the world. The knowledge I gained through that experience lead me to understand the need for more comprehensive, accesible, and inclusive design in the world.

My interests include travel, reading a good book, and singing emo music in the car with my partner.
